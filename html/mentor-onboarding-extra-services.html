What Mentoring Covers

One $30 session, every milestone

Under the flat $30/session model, resume and LinkedIn work is no longer a separate paid service. It sits inside regular mentoring. Mentees can use a session for any of the milestones below.

Career direction Map skills to specific Australian grad roles. Build a focused shortlist.
Resume sharpening Annotated feedback. Rewrite bullets to mirror target role language.
LinkedIn optimisation Headline, About, keyword strategy. Cold message templates.
Networking & outreach Cold message scripts and follow-up sequences that get referrals.
Interview practice Behavioural, technical, case formats. Live feedback.
Assessment centres Run-throughs of the formats firms actually use. Group coaching.
