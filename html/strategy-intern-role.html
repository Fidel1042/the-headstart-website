Strategy Intern

Apply now

About the Role

Want to actually apply your business and consulting knowledge to real growth strategy, while receiving professional career advice and gaining access to our pool of mentors?

This role focuses on analysing competitors, understanding student behaviour and helping shape growth strategies that improve how we reach university students 📈

What You'll Be Doing

  • Content Ideation 💡
    Generate LinkedIn post ideas using our content templates and themes
  • Consumer & Competitor Analysis 🔍
    Analyse competitors by reviewing their platforms as a student user and identify what works well
  • Strategy Development 🧠
    Work with the co-founders to understand the target audience and develop growth strategies
  • Weekly Strategy Review 📊
    Join weekly WIP meetings to review results, share insights and refine strategy

Minimum Requirements

  • Current business, marketing or related university student in Australia
  • Must have experience job or internship searching
  • Basic understanding of LinkedIn and professional content
  • Strong curiosity about marketing, strategy or startups

Standout Candidates

  • Able to commit around 5 hours per week consistently
  • Fast responder (messages replied within ~2 hours)
  • Ambitious and actively pursuing internships or graduate roles
  • Communicates clearly and manages workload well

What You'll Gain

  • Ad hoc career advice via WhatsApp to help you secure internships or graduate roles 💼
  • Structured weekly sessions on how to plan your career and get hired
  • Access to a network of mentors across multiple industries
  • Opportunity to contribute to real business growth strategies you can showcase on your resume 🚀

Internship Structure

  • 4-month internship
  • Around 5 hours per week including weekly WIP meetings
  • Remote or Melbourne-based with flexible working
  • Potential paid part-time opportunity based on performance ⭐
