Professional Standards & Boundaries

Platform Boundaries

  • Mentors must not take clients privately or accept payments outside the platform.
  • If a student requests this, report it immediately to Fidel or Koko.
  • Any breach (taking clients privately) will result in removal.
  • If students message you for advice, direct them to book a session.
  • Advice should be delivered within sessions, not through ongoing messaging.

Communication with Founders

WhatsApp Fidel – 0402 238 701

Reach out for:

  • Payment issues
  • Job search guidance support
  • Mentee mismatch
  • Any uncertainties or edge cases

Mentor Visibility

  • You may include The Headstart on your LinkedIn profile.
  • If a client specifically requests you, you will be prioritised for matching.
