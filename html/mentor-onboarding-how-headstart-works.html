How The Headstart Works

Customer Journey

How a typical mentee's journey would look like:

1
Initial Consultation
Mentee discovers Headstart Mentoring via LinkedIn, Instagram, or word of mouth. They book a free initial consultation. Koko runs the call to understand their current situation and needs.
2
Mentee Signs Up
If the mentee is ready to commit, they sign up to one of our plans:

1 Session — $110/session (pay as you go)
5 Sessions — $90/session (most popular)
Unlimited — $2,000 for 6 months (unlimited sessions)
3
Mentor Match
We match the mentee with a mentor within the same or similar industry.
4
WhatsApp Group Opened
A WhatsApp group is created between the mentee, their mentor, and Fidel. This is the main communication channel for scheduling, updates, and support throughout the mentoring relationship.
5
Sessions Begin
1-on-1 mentoring sessions run on an agreed schedule. The mentor logs each session in Teachworks within 24 hours of it occurring.
6
Progress & Ongoing Support
The mentee progresses through their goals. Additional sessions are booked as needed. If any issues arise, message Fidel directly on WhatsApp.
7
Outcome
Mentee lands their internship or graduate role. The ideal outcome is a mentee who is confident, well-prepared, and refers friends to Headstart.
