Early Career Mentor

If you've recently secured an entry-level role in business or tech as an international student, you know how confusing and uncertain that journey can feel. That's exactly why mentoring matters.

Who this is for (please only apply if you meet all criteria):

  • Recently hired in a junior/graduate role (graduated within the last 3 years)
  • Previously an international student at an Australian university
  • Currently working in a business or tech-related role

Imagine guiding someone through the same path you just navigated, helping them build confidence and land the role they once thought was out of reach. It is impact you can actually see.

Build leadership skills, make a real difference, and earn flexible side income alongside your full-time role. That is why you would want to apply as a mentor for The Headstart.

Qualification Process

1
Application Press 'Apply Now' to apply.
2
Phone screening You will be reached out for a 5 minute chat for you to introduce yourself.
3
Qualification interview 1: Background, Motivation & Commitment To understand your background, personal journey, and motivation to mentor. We will also discuss your availability and commitment.
4
Qualification interview 2: Mentor Capability & Value Creation To assess your ability to think strategically and coach effectively. We also evaluate how you create real outcomes for mentees.
5
Offer Congratulations, you have officially passed our qualification process. You are ready to be a Headstart Mentor.
6
Onboarding call Complete the Headstart Onboarding Module. Reach out if you have any questions.
