Session Logs & Payment

1. Logging Sessions

Headstart uses a simple Airtable form to log sessions. Save the form link as a bookmark. After every session, submit the form within 24 hours of it ending.

1.1  The Session Log Form

This is the only record Fidel uses to calculate your payment. There is nothing else to set up: no platform login, no schedule entry, just the form after each session.

Open Session Log Form →

1.2  How to Log a Session

  1. 1 Open the form (bookmark it for easy access).
  2. 2 Fill in the session details.
  3. 3 Submit within 24 hours of the session ending.
Log every session within 24 hours. Missing or late entries will delay your payment.

2. Payment

2.1  Your Pay Structure

Headstart sources the mentee, runs the consultation, and matches them to you. You receive your agreed per-session rate. Your contract covers the full terms, this is a quick refresher.

What's Included

The infrastructure that stands behind you on every mentee:

  • Operations handled. Payments are calculated and paid bi-weekly. Rescheduling, cancellations, and awkward client conversations go through Fidel, not you.
  • Founder backup on difficult cases. Mentees who push back, ghost, demand refunds, or behave inappropriately get escalated to Fidel and Koko. You are not carrying that weight alone.
  • Brand credibility. You get to operate under the Headstart name on LinkedIn, in content, and in conversations with prospective mentees.
  • Inbound pipeline. Headstart's marketing (LinkedIn, Instagram, SEO, referrals) brings in mentees you would not have reached on your own.

2.2  How You Get Paid

Payments are managed manually by Fidel on a bi-weekly basis. He uses the Airtable form submissions to confirm which sessions have been logged, then calculates and sends each mentor's payment directly.

If you have any questions about a payment, message Fidel on WhatsApp.

2.3  ABN Setup

You are paid as a contractor, not an employee. You need an Australian Business Number (ABN) to receive payment legally.

  1. 1 Go to abr.gov.au and click "Apply for an ABN"
  2. 2 Select entity type: Sole Trader
  3. 3 For business activity, enter: mentoring services or educational consulting
  4. 4 Submit. Your ABN is issued instantly in most cases
Register your ABN before your first session. Without it, Australian tax law requires us to withhold 47% of your payment.

2.4  Tax

As a contractor, you are responsible for your own tax. Headstart pays you the full agreed amount without withholding tax, provided your ABN is on file.

Set aside a portion of your earnings for tax and lodge your own return at the end of the financial year. Visit ato.gov.au for guidance on sole trader tax obligations.
