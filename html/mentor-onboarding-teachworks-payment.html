Teachworks & Payment

1. Teachworks

Teachworks is the platform Headstart uses to track sessions and calculate mentor payments. You will receive a login from Fidel before your first session.

1.1  Managing Schedules

Session times are agreed with your mentee over WhatsApp first. Once confirmed, enter the session into Teachworks before it occurs so it is on the official record.

If you need to reschedule, notify the mentee and Fidel on WhatsApp as early as possible, then update Teachworks accordingly.

1.2  How to Log a Session

After every session, mark it as complete in Teachworks within 24 hours. This is how Headstart tracks what sessions have been completed and what each mentor is owed.

Log every session within 24 hours. Missing this will cause your payment record to be inaccurate.

Step-by-step screenshots will be added here once the platform is set up.

2. Payment

2.1  How You Get Paid

Payments are managed manually by Fidel on a bi-weekly basis. He uses Teachworks to confirm which sessions have been logged, then calculates and sends each mentor's payment directly.

If you have any questions about a payment, message Fidel on WhatsApp.

2.2  ABN Setup

You are paid as a contractor, not an employee. You need an Australian Business Number (ABN) to receive payment legally.

  1. 1 Go to abr.gov.au and click "Apply for an ABN"
  2. 2 Select entity type: Sole Trader
  3. 3 For business activity, enter: mentoring services or educational consulting
  4. 4 Submit. Your ABN is issued instantly in most cases
Register your ABN before your first session. Without it, Australian tax law requires us to withhold 47% of your payment.

2.3  Tax

As a contractor, you are responsible for your own tax. Headstart pays you the full agreed amount without withholding tax, provided your ABN is on file.

Set aside a portion of your earnings for tax and lodge your own return at the end of the financial year. Visit ato.gov.au for guidance on sole trader tax obligations.
