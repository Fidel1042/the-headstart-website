  2. 2 Fill in the session details.

Log every session within 24 hours. Missing or late entries will delay your payment.

2. Payment

2.1  How You Get Paid

Payments are managed manually by Fidel on a bi-weekly basis. He uses the Airtable form submissions to confirm which sessions have been logged, then calculates and sends each mentor's payment directly.

If you have any questions about a payment, message Fidel on WhatsApp.

2.2  ABN Setup

You are paid as a contractor, not an employee. You need an Australian Business Number (ABN) to receive payment legally.

  1. 1 Go to abr.gov.au and click "Apply for an ABN"
  2. 2 Select entity type: Sole Trader
  3. 3 For business activity, enter: mentoring services or educational consulting
  4. 4 Submit. Your ABN is issued instantly in most cases
Register your ABN before your first session. Without it, Australian tax law requires us to withhold 47% of your payment.

2.3  Tax

As a contractor, you are responsible for your own tax. Headstart pays you the full agreed amount without withholding tax, provided your ABN is on file.

Set aside a portion of your earnings for tax and lodge your own return at the end of the financial year. Visit ato.gov.au for guidance on sole trader tax obligations.
