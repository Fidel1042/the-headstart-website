Marketing Intern

Apply now

About the Role

Want to actually apply your marketing knowledge and make a real impact, while receiving professional career advice and gaining access to our pool of mentors?

Look no further than joining Headstart Mentoring as our Marketing and Growth Intern!

What you will be doing

  • Content Execution 📱
    Create and publish consistent Instagram and LinkedIn content using our templates and brand guidelines
  • Creative Ownership 💡
    Let your creativity take over! Share your ideas and bring new content concepts to life, including creating different formats of contents!
  • Growth Strategy 📈
    Work with marketers and growth strategies to understand the target audience and generate growth strategies

Minimum Requirements

  • Current business student in Australia
  • Love creating content (personal page, club, project etc.)
  • Ambitious, ready to learn and own a part of the work

Standout Candidates

  • Able to commit around 5 hours per week (flexibility applies)
  • Responsible communicator
  • Ambitious and actively pursuing internships or graduate roles
  • Able to communicate workload clearly and manage expectations

What You'll Gain

  • Career advice to help you land internships or graduate roles 💼
  • Consistent feedback on your work
  • Access to a network of mentors across multiple industries
  • Opportunity to create measurable marketing impact you can showcase on your resume 📈

Internship Structure

  • 16 week internship
  • Around 5 hours per week including a weekly progress meeting
  • Remote or Melbourne-based with flexible working
  • Paid part-time opportunity based on impact and performance
